Ishchemic Heart Disease is still considered the top cause of DALYs in the United States. With a large population of the relatively young population being diagnosed as a patient of either of the three classes of heart disease – Myocardial Infarction, Anginal or Coronary Artery disease and stroke, this is a disease burden that has not decreased despite expectations. Though Heart Disease prevalence seems to be less in percentage, however, it still is the leading cause of death in the United States.
